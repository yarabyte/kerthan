Big-number statistic for the impact strip.

```jsx
<StatCard value="20" suffix="ans" label="d'expérience à Douala" />
<StatCard value="6" label="services médicaux" tone="gold" />
<StatCard value="24/7" label="urgences" tone="light" align="center" />
```

Use `tone="light"` on dark green panels. Props: `value`, `suffix`, `label`, `tone`, `align`.
